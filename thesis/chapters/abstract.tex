Managing cloud applications is hard. In order to deal with them, businesses use orchestration platforms. However, orchestration platforms often come with great overhead and high expenses in operating and maintaining it. To overcome related issue, our thesis aims to build a working prototype of a distributed workloads management system based on a pull-model, which will bring no overhead and be easy in deployment and operation.

\end{abstract}
